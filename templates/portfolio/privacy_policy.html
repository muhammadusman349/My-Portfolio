1. Introduction

Welcome to Muhammad Usman's portfolio website. This privacy policy explains how I collect, use, and protect your information when you visit my website (usmanportfolio.com) and interact with my services. 2. Information We Collect

When you visit my portfolio website and interact with its features, I may collect the following information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and message content when you submit the contact form
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and referring website

I do not collect sensitive personal information unless you voluntarily provide it through the contact form.

3. How We Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes:

  • Communication: Respond to your inquiries, messages, and project-related questions

I will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

I take the security of your personal information seriously. As a professional software developer, I implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure Hosting: The website uses secure hosting with SSL encryption (HTTPS)
  • Data Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to the contact form submissions
  • Regular Updates: Security measures and software are regularly updated to address vulnerabilities
  • Backup Security: Data backups are stored securely and encrypted

While I strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. However, I am committed to implementing and maintaining appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk.

5. Your Rights

Under certain circumstances, you have rights under data protection laws in relation to your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data

  • Object to processing of your personal data

  • Request transfer of your personal data

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, my data practices, or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact me:

  • Email: Contact me through the contact form on this website
  • Professional Inquiries: For business opportunities, collaborations, or project discussions

I will respond to your inquiries within a reasonable timeframe and work to address your concerns promptly.
